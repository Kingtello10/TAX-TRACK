Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

TaxTrack NG ("we", "our", "us") is committed to safeguarding your personal and financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide when using our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email address, and phone number
  • Tax and financial data: VAT, PAYE, consumption records
  • Uploaded receipts or CSV files for processing
  • Technical data: IP address, device info, browser type

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used to:

  • Generate accurate tax calculations and reports
  • Provide interactive dashboards for your tax data
  • Enhance platform functionality, performance, and security

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. Sensitive data is encrypted and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, OCR processing, or payment integration. All providers adhere to strict data protection policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data where applicable by law. Contact us at support@taxtrack.ng for any requests.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. All changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to us at support@taxtrack.ng.
